# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: 2026-04-22**

Welcome to RoadLyrics (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, stores, and protects your information.

### 1. Information Collection and Use

#### 1.1 Music Playback Data

The App accesses information related to the currently playing content, including:

- Currently playing song title, artist, and album name
- Playback progress and playback state
- Album artwork
- Song identifiers provided by music services

This data is used primarily to display current playback information and synced lyrics to connected devices on your personal hotspot network. To fetch lyrics, the App may send the song information required for lyrics matching to third-party lyrics service providers. Other than processing required for these features, the App does not operate its own server to collect, store, or analyze your playback data. Limited usage analytics and crash diagnostics are handled separately through Firebase as described in Section 1.8.

#### 1.2 Media Library Access

The App requires access to your Apple Music media library to retrieve current playback information. This permission is used solely to read playback status and will not modify your media library.

#### 1.3 Spotify Integration

If you choose to connect Spotify, the App will:

- Connect your Spotify account through Spotify's official authorization flow
- Store the authorization information required to connect Spotify locally on your device
- Only access current playback status and basic playback controls

Spotify authorization information is used only to connect to Spotify services and is not shared with unrelated third parties.

#### 1.4 Lyrics Retrieval

To provide lyrics display functionality, the App sends necessary song information to third-party lyrics services to retrieve matching lyrics.

Data sent to lyrics services is usually limited to the information needed to match lyrics, such as song name, artist name, album name, or playback progress. The App does not intentionally send your name, email address, phone number, or similar personally identifying information to lyrics service providers.

#### 1.5 Local Network Service

The App provides a local access page on your device to display music playback information and lyrics to connected devices on your personal hotspot network. This local access feature:

- Is only accessible within your personal hotspot LAN
- Is used only to display the lyrics page to devices you allow to connect to your hotspot
- Is not provided as a public internet service

#### 1.6 Device Preferences

The App stores your interface preferences (such as theme, font size, lyrics display options, etc.) locally on your device. This data does not leave your device.

#### 1.7 Trial Period Management

The App stores trial usage records locally on your device, such as usage duration and reset dates, to ensure fair use of the trial period. This data:

- Is stored only on your device
- Does not contain any personally identifiable information
- Is not transmitted to any external server
- May remain on the device even after the App is uninstalled

#### 1.8 Firebase Analytics and Crash Reporting

The App uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics, services provided by Google, to understand how the App is used, diagnose errors and crashes, measure reliability, and improve the App.

Usage analytics may include:

- App usage duration, music source category, connection step, successful or abandoned connection state, connected browser/device category, lyrics availability status, and lyrics preview setting changes
- Error diagnostics such as sanitized error context, error domain, error code, provider name, operation name, status code, and reason
- Technical metadata generated by Firebase, such as app version, OS version, device model, language/region, timestamps, and Firebase installation identifiers

Crash reports may include crash stack traces, crash timestamps, app and OS version, device model, and Firebase or Crashlytics installation identifiers.

The App does not intentionally collect or log song titles, artist names, lyrics content, Spotify authorization tokens, Apple Music tokens, IP addresses, or raw browser identifiers in Firebase Analytics events or app-provided crash diagnostic fields. Analytics event parameters are sanitized before being sent.

The App includes a "Share Usage Analytics" setting. In regions where consent is required, the App asks for permission before enabling usage analytics. In other regions, usage analytics may be enabled by default, and you can disable it in iOS Settings > RoadLyrics > Share Usage Analytics. When usage analytics is disabled, the App disables Firebase Analytics collection and resets Firebase Analytics data on the device.

Firebase data is transmitted to and processed by Google according to Google's Firebase and Google Analytics terms and privacy practices, including:

- [Firebase Privacy and Security](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google Analytics Terms of Service](https://www.google.com/analytics/terms/)
- [Firebase Crashlytics Terms](https://firebase.google.com/terms/crashlytics/)

### 2. VPN Functionality Disclosure

**Important:** The App uses local VPN functionality to help connected devices on your personal hotspot access the lyrics display page. It is **not** designed to provide internet privacy protection, traffic encryption, or anonymous browsing.

The App's VPN functionality:

- Is used **only** to create a local access channel on your personal hotspot network, allowing connected devices to access the lyrics display page
- Does **not** route, intercept, monitor, or modify your internet traffic
- Does **not** send your traffic to any external server or proxy
- Does **not** change your IP address or geographic location
- Does **not** collect or analyze your network activity

VPN-related processing is used only to support local lyrics page access. It is not used to proxy, analyze, or forward your internet traffic.

### 3. Data Storage

| Data Type | Storage Location | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Playback state | Device memory | Runtime only, cleared on exit |
| UI settings | Local device storage | Until you uninstall the App or manually reset |
| Spotify authorization information | Local device storage | Retained while the connection remains valid; reauthorization may be required after expiration |
| Trial records | Local secure device storage | May be retained long-term to manage the trial period |
| Lyrics cache | Device memory | Runtime only, cleared on exit |
| Firebase Analytics events | Google Firebase / Google Analytics | Retained according to Google/Firebase retention settings and policies |
| Crash diagnostics | Google Firebase Crashlytics | Retained according to Firebase Crashlytics policies |

### 4. Data Sharing

The App does **not**:

- Sell, rent, or exchange your personal information with any third party
- Collect or transmit device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, etc.) for advertising purposes
- Use Firebase data for personalized advertising, user profiling, or targeted advertising
- Share song titles, artist names, lyrics content, Spotify authorization tokens, Apple Music tokens, IP addresses, or raw browser identifiers with Firebase as intentional analytics or app-provided diagnostic data

The App shares limited usage analytics and crash diagnostics with Google Firebase / Google Analytics as described in Section 1.8. The App may also send song information to lyrics service providers as described in Section 1.4.

### 5. Data Security

The App takes the following measures to protect your data:

- The local access page is intended only for devices connected to your personal hotspot
- Trial period data is stored using system-provided secure storage
- Firebase Analytics is configured with ad storage, ad user data, and ad personalization disabled
- Analytics event names and parameters are limited and sanitized before being sent
- No remote database or user account system is maintained

### 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

### 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be made available within the App or on the App Store page. Your continued use of the App constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
